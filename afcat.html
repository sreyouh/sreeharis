* Flying Branch & Ground Duty (Non-Tech): GA, English, Numerical Ability, Reasoning
* Ground Duty (Technical): AFCAT + Engineering Knowledge Test (EKT)

AFCAT Examination Overview

AFCAT is conducted by the Indian Air Force to select officers for Flying, Ground Duty (Technical), and Ground Duty (Non-Technical) branches. The exam tests awareness, aptitude, numerical skills, and officer-like reasoning.
